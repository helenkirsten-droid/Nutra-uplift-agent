üìã Key Model Assumptions & Methodology

This model calculates uplift through a five-stage funnel:

1. Media Reach: Based on actual media plan delivering 1.2M unique reach across BVOD, CTV, OOH, Audio, and Sampling
2. Brand Awareness: Attention rate (default 45%) determines how many people actually notice the campaign. Industry benchmark: 40-50% for premium media.
3. Consideration: Of those aware, 30% move into active consideration (informed by awareness-to-consideration ratios from IPA Databank)
4. Traffic & Conversion: Brand awareness drives website traffic lift AND improves conversion rates through increased trust and familiarity
5. Revenue Impact: Three sources combine - traffic lift, CVR improvement, and direct sampling conversions

Attribution Note: Some overlap exists between traffic and CVR sources (brand awareness impacts both). The model shows gross impact; net impact may be 10-15% lower due to attribution overlap.

Time Lag: This shows in-period impact (May-Aug 2026). Brand awareness built will continue driving sales in subsequent months, with estimated 6-12 month halo effect worth an additional 30-50% of in-period impact.
